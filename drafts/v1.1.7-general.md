## Noxaudit v1.1.7 is live

Hi everyone,

We’ve just released Noxaudit v1.1.7! This update focuses on giving you more visibility into your project health, ensuring that no stone is left unturned during your audits.

### What’s improved
- **Flagging missing tests**: If a project doesn't have any test files, Noxaudit will now create a finding to let you know.

### Why this matters
Code that isn't tested is code that might behave unexpectedly. By highlighting when test files are missing, we’re helping you maintain high standards for reliability and security. It’s a simple change that makes it much harder for gaps in your testing suite to go unnoticed.

Thanks for using Noxaudit! We're excited to keep making your auditing process shorter and more thorough.
