## Noxaudit v1.1.8 is here

We’ve just released version 1.1.8, and we’re excited to share a major update that expands how you can use Noxaudit to secure your projects.

### What’s New

- **Full Dart Support**: We have added Dart support across all focus areas in Noxaudit. Whether you are building mobile apps with Flutter or working on server-side Dart projects, you can now get the same deep insights and auditing you rely on for other languages.

### Why this matters

Our goal is to make security auditing as seamless as possible for every developer. By bringing Dart into the fold, we’re ensuring that developers in the Flutter and Dart ecosystems have the tools they need to stay secure without any extra configuration or friction.

Thanks for being part of the Noxaudit community. We can't wait to see what you build with these new capabilities!
