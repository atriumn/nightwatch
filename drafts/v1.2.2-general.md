## Noxaudit 1.2.2 is here

We’ve just released version 1.2.2 of Noxaudit, focusing on making your experience smoother and more efficient under the hood.

### What’s New
- **Refined Data Processing**: We’ve optimized how the system prepares your data for auditing. By adjusting our processing "headroom," the app now works more efficiently without wasting resources.
- **Snappier Performance**: These changes mean less idle time and a more responsive experience when handling large tasks.

### Why this matters
Our goal is to keep Noxaudit fast and reliable. By fine-tuning how the software handles information in the early stages of an audit, we’re ensuring that your results are processed as quickly as possible.

You can find the full release details on our [GitHub](https://github.com/atriumn/noxaudit/releases/tag/v1.2.2).

Thanks for being part of the community!
