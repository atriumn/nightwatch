## A smoother experience with v1.1.5

Noxaudit v1.1.5 is now available. This update focuses on making the tool feel more responsive and dependable for your daily tasks.

### What's improved

- **Faster performance:** We’ve optimized the way data is handled so you spend less time waiting for screens to load.
- **Interface cleanup:** We've polished several parts of the dashboard to make navigation more intuitive and clear.
- **Better stability:** Under-the-hood improvements ensure that everything works exactly as expected every time you log in.

### Why it matters

The less time you spend thinking about the tool, the more time you have for the actual audit. These updates are designed to get out of your way and help you work more efficiently.

You can find the full details of this release on [GitHub](https://github.com/atriumn/noxaudit/releases).

Thanks for being part of the community!
