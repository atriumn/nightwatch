## Noxaudit 1.2.4 is live

Hello everyone,

We just released version 1.2.4 of Noxaudit! This update is all about making the tool more efficient so you can get through your audits with less friction.

### What’s improved

- **Faster initial processing:** We have optimized the way Noxaudit handles the first pass of your data. This makes the scanning process snappier and more reliable.
- **Performance tuning:** We’ve streamlined our internal workflows to ensure you get the best results without unnecessary delays.

Our goal is to keep Noxaudit running as smoothly as possible so you can focus on the results. You can find the full details of this release on GitHub:
https://github.com/atriumn/noxaudit/releases/tag/v1.2.4
