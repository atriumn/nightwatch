## Noxaudit 1.1.9 is here!

We just pushed some improvements to make your auditing experience more stable and reliable. This update focuses on making sure your work keeps moving, even when external services have a hiccup.

### What’s New

- **Better handling of connection glitches:** We’ve improved how the system handles temporary API errors. Instead of stopping or crashing during a large batch, Noxaudit now automatically retries the connection, keeping your audits on track.
- **Refined data retrieval:** We updated our core provider logic to ensure that fetching information is more consistent and less prone to errors.

### Why this matters

You should spend less time restarting failed tasks and more time focusing on the results. These fixes ensure that even if there is a brief network blip, Noxaudit stays resilient and finishes the job.

Thanks for being part of the community! If you have any feedback on this version, feel free to reach out.
