## Version 1.1.6 is now live

We’ve just released the latest version of Noxaudit, focusing on helping you identify potential risks more effectively and making our resources easier to navigate.

### Spotting blind spots automatically
Testing is a cornerstone of secure software. To help you maintain high standards across your organization, Noxaudit now automatically flags repositories that lack tests as high-severity findings. This change makes it easier to see at a glance which projects might need extra attention to ensure they are stable and secure.

### A fresh look for our documentation
We want it to be as easy as possible for you to get the most out of Noxaudit. We’ve moved our documentation to a new platform that is faster and more intuitive, ensuring you can find the answers you need without the friction.

### What changed in v1.1.6:
- **High-severity alerts** for repositories missing test suites.
- **New documentation site** for a better reading and search experience.
- **General refinements** to keep the platform running smoothly.

Thank you for being a part of the Noxaudit community!
