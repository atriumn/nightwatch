## Noxaudit 1.2.0 is here!

We are excited to share that version 1.2.0 has officially landed. This update focuses on making your experience snappier and more responsive, ensuring you get the information you need without the wait.

### What’s new?

The core of this update is a significant improvement to how we handle initial audit checks. Here is what you can expect:

- **Instant Feedback:** We have moved away from delayed batch processing for our initial scans. This means your audits start moving immediately, giving you results much faster than before.
- **Smoother Workflow:** By streamlining how we communicate with our analysis tools, the entire app feels more responsive from the moment you hit start.
- **General Polish:** We have included various small fixes to ensure everything runs reliably behind the scenes.

### Why it matters

You shouldn't have to wait around for your data. By speeding up the "pre-pass" phase of our audits, we are helping you identify potential issues earlier in your process, saving you time and effort.

Thank you for using Noxaudit to keep your projects secure!
