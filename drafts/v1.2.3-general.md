Hi there,

We’re excited to share that Noxaudit v1.2.3 is now live! This update focuses on making the tool more capable when handling your most detailed work.

## More room for your results
The headline change in this release is a significant increase in our capacity for generating content. We’ve quadrupled the limit for how much information the system can provide in a single response.

### Why this matters to you
- **Comprehensive Audits:** You can now generate much longer reports without worrying about the system cutting off mid-way.
- **Deeper Analysis:** With more space to work, Noxaudit can provide more thorough explanations and insights for complex tasks.
- **Better Flow:** Less time spent re-running prompts means a smoother, faster workflow for you.

This update is all about ensuring that no matter how big the task, Noxaudit has the room to deliver exactly what you need.

You can find the full technical release notes on [GitHub](https://github.com/atriumn/noxaudit/releases).

Thanks for using Noxaudit!
