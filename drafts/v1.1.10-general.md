## Big files? No problem.

We’ve just released **Noxaudit 1.1.10**, and it’s designed to make your auditing process even smoother, especially when you're working with extensive documents.

### What’s improved?

- **Automatic Smart Scanning:** Sometimes a file is just too big for the AI to read all at once. Noxaudit now automatically recognizes these situations and adjusts how it processes the data. This means you’ll get complete results without having to manually tweak any settings.

### Why this helps you

This update removes the guesswork when dealing with long files. Whether your audit is a few pages or a massive report, Noxaudit will scale its efforts to ensure nothing is overlooked. It’s all about giving you peace of mind that the tool is working for you, not against you.
